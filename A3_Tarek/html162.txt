<p>The Mets tweeted:</p>
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet">
<p dir="ltr" lang="en">Second show added. <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/CitiField?src=hash">#CitiField</a> <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/Mets?src=hash">#Mets</a> <a href="https://t.co/9L7nGFqrwR">https://t.co/9L7nGFqrwR</a></p>
<p>— New York Mets (@Mets) <a href="https://twitter.com/Mets/status/697422441119903744">February 10, 2016</a></p></blockquote>
<p>The response came fast and furious:</p>

<p>— Ed O. (@Ed_OM_) <a href="https://twitter.com/Ed_OM_/status/697422998551339008">February 10, 2016</a></p></blockquote>
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet">
<p dir="ltr" lang="en"><a href="https://twitter.com/Mets">@Mets</a> <a href="https://twitter.com/CitiField">@CitiField</a> <a href="https://twitter.com/Beyonce">@Beyonce</a> boycotting citifield this year if you let this cop hater play there</p>
<p>— Frankie Carbone (@FrankieBanks101) <a href="https://twitter.com/FrankieBanks101/status/697425049268195328">February 10, 2016</a></p></blockquote>
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet"><p><a href="https://twitter.com/Mets">@Mets</a> <a href="https://twitter.com/CitiField">@CitiField</a> sorry no one in my family or friends will be coming to your ball park as long as she performs.</p>

<blockquote class="twitter-tweet">
