</div><h2 style="text-align: center;">Corn on the Cob Day</h2>
<p><a href="http://lifewithheidi.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/488320_small.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-123444 size-full" src="http://lifewithheidi.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/488320_small.jpg" alt="Corn on the Cob Day" width="700" height="467" srcset="http://www.lifewithheidi.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/488320_small-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.lifewithheidi.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/488320_small.jpg 700w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px" /></a></p>
<p>Each day of the year is often times a food holiday that is unique and fun to celebrate. So I&#8217;ve done the research to bring you a fun food holiday each day!!</p>
<p>Corn on the Cob Day is celebrated each year on June 11th!! &nbsp;While this is a delicious treat, here in Missouri it isn&#8217;t quite ready to go just yet. &nbsp;But there is always the frozen or store bought kind to help us out in a pinch!! But my favorite corn on the cob comes out of a garden locally grown!! We have about another month to wait for this delicious food to set on. &nbsp;So in the meantime I guess I will dream about the first ear of corn!!&nbsp;</p>
<p>Enjoy yours with butter, salt and of course a little pepper!! &nbsp;</p>
<p>Happy Corn on the Cob Day!&nbsp;</p>
