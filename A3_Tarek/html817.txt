<div class="subscriber-only"><div id="_idContainer013" class="Basic-Text-Frame"><p class="Body-Text_Body-text-RR">The Episcopal Church is the American outpost in the Anglican Communion, with about 2 million members. There are 80 million Anglicans worldwide. Marin County supports several Episcopal churches and, of course, there is the ever-beloved Grace Cathedral that stands tall on San Francisco’s Nob Hill.</p><p class="Body-Text_Body-text-RR">Anyway, the Episcopal Church extends the sacrament of marriage to same-sex couples. This a no-no with the wider Anglican Communion and this month that bigger body flexed its muscle by putting Episcopalians in, for lack of a better term, an ecclesiastical time out, removing the American church from Anglican Communion decision making for three years.</p><p class="Body-Text_Body-text-RR">No one knows how it will end up. But your Marin Forum compiler thinks Marin residents root for the position of Pope Paul VI.
