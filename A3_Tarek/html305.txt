		<div class="entry-hdr"><h1 class="entry-title">Songs That Make Me Want To Write Songs</h1></div>

		<div class="entry-meta">
						<div class="meta-info-wrap post_hide_date entry-meta-icons">
			<span class="sep posted-on">Posted on </span><a href="http://blog.roadsideattraction.com/songs-make-want-write-songs.html" title="5:41 pm" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-04-02T17:41:17+00:00">April 2, 2014</time></a><span class="by-author"> <span class="sep"> by </span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.roadsideattraction.com/author/phil-johnson" title="View all posts by Phil Johnson" rel="author">Phil Johnson</a><div style="display: none"><a href="/?eSShWW">Phil Johnson</a></div></span></span><span class="updated">April 17, 2014</span>
			</div><!-- .entry-meta-icons -->		</div><!-- .entry-meta -->
	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content cf">
		<div style="width: 269px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><img src="http://i0.wp.com/upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/2f/Gogol_Bordello_-_Rock_in_Rio_Madrid_2012_-_55.jpg?resize=259%2C259" alt=""  data-recalc-dims="1"><p class="wp-caption-text">Eugene Hutz of Gogol Bordello</p></div>
<p>There are certain songs that make me want to write. Maybe it&#8217;s a great lyric, or a killer riff, or a neat production idea, or a cool groove.&nbsp; Something about the songs makes me want to pick up my guitar and write a song.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Usually I hear those songs on shuffle with everything else and think &#8220;I need to make a list of those songs so I can have a go-to fountain of inspiration.&#8221;&nbsp; And so I have.&nbsp; And I thought it would be fun to share them with you.</p>
<p>&nbsp;You&#8217;ll notice that there are very few comedy songs on here.&nbsp; I enjoy a lot of those, of course.&nbsp; But most of the inspiration for my own comedy music comes from regular songs.&nbsp; And while there are definitely some big hits in there (they&#8217;re hits for a reason), there&#8217;s some pretty obscure stuff in here too.</p>
<p>My personal playlist on my laptop actually has 220 songs.&nbsp; But Spotify only had 165 of them for some reason.&nbsp; For instance &#8220;Psycho Girl&#8221; by the Alley Boys and &#8220;Eleanor Rigby&#8221; by the Beatles aren&#8217;t on there.&nbsp; But it&#8217;ll give you a good idea of what goes on in my head.</p>
<p>And yes, one of my songs is on there.&nbsp; &#8220;Afrodizzyac&#8221; is one on my proudest achievements as a songwriter, so I get juiced up when I hear it. <img src="http://i0.wp.com/blog.roadsideattraction.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png?w=940" alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;" data-recalc-dims="1" />&nbsp; It&#8217;s only on top because I was going through the songs in alphabetical order.</p>
<p>So, without further ado&#8230; These are the songs that make me want to write songs!</p>
