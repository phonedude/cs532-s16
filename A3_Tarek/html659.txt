                <div class="post-excerpt"><p><a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/tag/top-10-social-media-blog/" target="_blank"><img class="alignright" title="blogs" alt="" width="164" height="167" srcset="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/images/case-study-pose.png, http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/images/case-study-pose@2x.png 2x" /></a> Are you looking for some great social media blogs to add to your daily reading? Look no further!</p>
<p>Our <a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/nominate-your-favorite-social-media-blog-7th-annual-top-10-social-media-blog-contest/" target="_blank">seventh-annual social media blog contest</a> generated over 300 nominations. A panel of experts carefully reviewed the <a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/finalists-top-10-social-media-blogs-2016/" target="_blank">20 finalists</a> based on their content quality, post frequency and reader involvement.</p>
<p><strong>Here are the top 10 social media blogs you should add to your regular reading list in 2016.</strong></p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img alt="2016 top ten social media blog contest" width="600" height="315" srcset="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-top-10-winners-2016-600.png, http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-top-10-winners-2016-600@2x.png 2x" /><p class="wp-caption-text">Discover the top 10 social media blogs for 2016.</p></div>
<p><span id="more-93008"></span></p>
<h3>#1: Buffer Social</h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.bufferapp.com/" target="_blank">Buffer Social</a> regularly addresses questions many marketers have, answering them with authoritative, in-depth articles on cutting-edge topics.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://blog.bufferapp.com/" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-buffersocial-top-10-2016.png" alt="buffer social blog" width="600" height="462" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Buffer Social offers useful thoughts on social media and marketing.</p></div>
<h3>#2: Jenn&#8217;s Trends</h3>
<p><a href="http://www.jennstrends.com/blog/" target="_blank">Jenn&#8217;s Trends</a> is laser-focused on Instagram, but occasionally offers tips on social media marketing strategy and tactics.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://www.jennstrends.com/blog" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-jennstrends-top-10-2016.png" alt="jenn's trends blog" width="600" height="544" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Jenn&#8217;s Trends is a must-read for marketers on Instagram.</p></div>
<h3>#3: Peg Fitzpatrick</h3>
<p><a href="http://pegfitzpatrick.com/blog/" target="_blank">Peg Fitzpatrick</a> combines resourceful content on a variety of social media platforms with practical advice for marketers.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://pegfitzpatrick.com/blog/" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-pegfitzpatrick-top-10-2016.png" alt="peg fitzpatrick blog" width="600" height="445" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Peg Fitzpatrick&#8217;s delivery style is both personable and accessible.</p></div>
<h3>#4: RazorSocial</h3>
<p><a href="http://www.razorsocial.com/blog/" target="_blank">RazorSocial</a> delivers well-researched and comprehensive content on social media tools.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://www.razorsocial.com/blog/" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-razorsocial-top-10-2016.png" alt="razor social blog" width="600" height="611" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">RazorSocial shares advice on using social media tools.</p></div>
<h3>#5: Rebekah Radice</h3>
<p><a href="http://rebekahradice.com/blog/" target="_blank">Rebekah Radice</a> uses plenty of examples to support content that teaches social media marketers about topics that are important to them.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://rebekahradice.com/blog/" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-rebekahradice-top-10-2016.png" alt="rebekah radice blog" width="600" height="714" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Reading Rebekah Radice is like having a one-on-one conversation with her.</p></div>
<h3>#6: Simply Measured</h3>
<p><a href="http://simplymeasured.com/blog/" target="_blank">Simply Measured</a> uses a data-driven, example-rich approach to help marketers understand best practices for using social media.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://simplymeasured.com/blog/" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-simplymeasured-top-10-2016.png" alt="simply measured blog" width="600" height="527" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Simply Measured provides well-written, interesting content.</p></div>
<h3>#7: Socially Sorted</h3>
<p><a href="http://sociallysorted.com.au/blog/" target="_blank">Socially Sorted</a> helps marketers learn to use all things related to visual content in social media.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://sociallysorted.com.au/blog/" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-sociallysorted-top-10-2016.png" alt="socially sorted blog" width="600" height="640" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Socially Sorted provides consistent value.</p></div>
<h3>#8: Sprout Social</h3>
<p><a href="http://sproutsocial.com/insights/" target="_blank">Sprout Social</a> publishes advice for marketers alongside insights from their own team.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://sproutsocial.com/insights/" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-sproutsocial-top-10-2016.png" alt="sprout social blog" width="600" height="607" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Sprout Social&#8217;s content is interesting and useful.</p></div>
<h3>#9: The Social Media Hat</h3>
<p><a href="http://thesocialmediahat.com/" target="_blank">The Social Media Hat</a> features authoritative content that takes no shortcuts with explanations of what to do and how to do it.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://thesocialmediahat.com/" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-thesocialmediahat-top-10-2016.png" alt="the social media hat blog" width="600" height="607" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">The Social Media Hat delivers their content with a lot of heart.</p></div>
<h3>#10: Unmetric</h3>
<p><a href="http://blog.unmetric.com/" target="_blank">Unmetric</a> uses an analytical approach to uncover and share surprising insights into social media marketing.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://blog.unmetric.com/" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-unmetric-top-10-2016.png" alt="unmetric blog" width="600" height="592" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Unmetric has some interesting takes on marketing.</p></div>
<p><strong style="font-size: 1rem;">Congratulations to the winners!</strong><span style="font-size: 1rem;"> Be sure to check out these amazing social media blogs.</span></p>
<p><strong><img class="alignright wp-image-74207" src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/ldj-top10blog-badge-2016.png" alt="social media examiner top 10 social media blog 2016 badge" width="108" height="164" />The badge of distinction</strong>: If you&#8217;re a winner, you can post the image you see here on your blog. Please link back to this page.</p>

<p>Special thanks to <strong><a href="http://www.annhandley.com/" target="_blank">Ann Handley</a></strong> (chief content officer for MarketingProfs, author of the book <i>Everybody Writes: Your Go-To Guide to Creating Ridiculously Good Content</i> and co-author of the best-selling <i>Content Rules</i>), <strong><a href="http://www.problogger.net/about-problogger/" target="_blank">Darren Rowse</a></strong> (founder of ProBlogger.net and author of <em>ProBlogger: Secrets for Blogging Your Way to a Six-Figure Income</em>) and<strong> <a href="http://maximizesocialbusiness.com/author/admin/" target="_blank">Neal Schaffer</a> </strong>(founder of Maximize Social Business and author of three books including <em>Maximize Your Social</em>).</p>
