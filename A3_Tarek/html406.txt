<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
Lent: Bishop Warns Christians Against Social Vices
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-1146420097240089557' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kciSprYDSEo/VrxM4u8s1UI/AAAAAAAAUPE/3vnje1azg-Q/s1600/bishop%2Bmakurdi.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="248" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kciSprYDSEo/VrxM4u8s1UI/AAAAAAAAUPE/3vnje1azg-Q/s400/bishop%2Bmakurdi.jpg" width="400" /></a></div>
<span style="font-size: large;">The Catholic Bishop of Makurdi Diocese, Wilfred Anagbe, has charged 
Christians to emulate Jesus Christ by sharing love and performing acts 
of charity during the Lenten period which began on Wednesday.</span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-size: large;">



</span><span style="font-size: large;">Speaking to the News Agency of Nigeria (NAN) in Makurdi, Anagbe said 
that lent, the 40-day fasting period by Christains, was a time for sober
 reflections and spiritual rejuvenation.</span><br />
<a name='more'></a><br />
<span style="font-size: large;">
</span><span style="font-size: large;">He charged Christians to continue to show faithfulness to God, and 
use the period, which began with the Ash Wednesday, to care for the 
less-privileged.</span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-size: large;">
</span><span style="font-size: large;">He commended the ongoing measures by President Muhammadu Buhari to 
revive the economy and warned Christians to refrain from dragging the 
church to the mud by indulging in acts of corruption.</span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-size: large;">
</span><span style="font-size: large;">According to the bishop, no true Christian will indulge in such 
shameful acts of looting the treasury or other acts of personal 
aggrandizement to the detriment of the wellbeing of other Nigerians.</span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-size: large;">
</span><span style="font-size: large;">He called on the faithful to be innovative and productive in their 
services to the country &#8220;for the purpose of uplifting the economy and 
transforming the lives of the people&#8217;&#8217;.</span><br />
<span style="font-size: large;">
</span><span style="font-size: large;">He warned against double standards by Christians and advised them to 
reflect their virtues in their work places and other activities.</span><br />
