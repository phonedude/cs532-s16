							<h2>EXCLUSIVE: Christian Collins Talks New Music, Social Media &#038; MORE</h2>
						</div>
						<div class="box-one">
														<p><p><center><a href="http://cdn1.images.popstaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/Christian-Headshot.jpg" class="lightbox" ><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-47442" src="http://cdn1.images.popstaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/Christian-Headshot.jpg" alt="" width="501" height="501" /></a></center>We caught up with Christian Collins to get the inside scoop on his budding music career! He told us all about his new single, &#8220;You Don&#8217;t Have To Go,&#8221; the inspiration behind it, and which artists he&#8217;s hoping to get in the studio with someday! Check out his Q&amp;A below and don&#8217;t forget to follow Christian on <span style="color: #ff00ff;"><a style="color: #ff00ff;" href="https://twitter.com/WeeklyChris" target="_blank">Twitter</a></span>! <img src="http://cdn1.images.popstaronline.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;" /></p>
<p>Watch the &#8220;You Don&#8217;t Have To Go&#8221; lyric video right here:</p>
<p><center><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/o2u2jaIGZ9w" width="560" height="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></center><strong>Love the new single! Can you tell us a bit about it/what inspired it?</strong><br />
The single was inspired by a girl getting lost looking for affirmation in the wrong places and letting her know she has somewhere safe and someone who cares for her.</p>
<p><strong>How does it feel to see such a great reaction to the song from all of your fans?</strong><br />
It&#8217;s an incredible feeling. I&#8217;m so blessed.</p>
<p><strong>How would you describe your sound?</strong><br />
I&#8217;ve been inspired by many great artists, in R&amp;B, Hip Hop, Classical, Pop, but I think my music is a portrait of me and my mind. I&#8217;m always working with different sounds, but in the end I want the music to reflect me. I want it to authentically reflect what I&#8217;m going through and the thoughts and concepts that run through my mind.</p>
<p><strong>What’s the song-writing process like for you?</strong><br />
It depends on the song. Sometimes I&#8217;ll have a concept in my mind and just start writing from there. Sometimes I&#8217;ll build a beat and freestyle melodies over it. It really varies.</p>
<p><strong>Who are some of your musical inspirations? Dream collab?</strong><br />
Some of My musical inspirations are The Weeknd, Bryson Tiller, Ed Sheeran, Drake, and Jon Bellion. It would be a dream to collab with any of these artists.</p>
<p><strong>What’s the most-played song on your iTunes at the moment?</strong><br />
Most played song is “Don&#8217;t” by Bryson Tiller</p>
<p><strong>What else can fans expect from you, musically? Any more originals coming?</strong><br />
Lots of new stuff coming soon. Working to release some new singles as well as the EP.</p>
<p><strong>How do you go about choosing a song to cover for YouTube?</strong><br />
If I feel like I can put my own spin on it, I&#8217;ll go for it.</p>
<p><strong>You have a huge following on social media – what’s the best way for a fan to get your attention on Twitter or Instagram?</strong><br />
Consistency. I always notice who&#8217;s constantly active <img src="http://cdn1.images.popstaronline.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;" /></p>
<p><strong>What’s something you’re hoping to cross off your bucket list this year?</strong><br />
Release an EP, tour with my music and meet as many people as possible.</p>
<p><strong>Do you have any current obsessions? (Ex: books/movies/tv shows)</strong><br />
I watch a lot of documentaries, my favorites are Living on One Dollar, Food Inc., Slingshot, The Hacker Wars and Ted Talks are awesome too!</p>
<p><strong>Any special message for your fans?</strong><br />
I am so thankful for all your support. I am so blessed. Thank you for helping make my dream come true. So much love!</p>
</p>
