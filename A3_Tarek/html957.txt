										<p style="text-align: left;"><a href="http://www.ezcare2.com/childcare-blog/wp-content/uploads/PicMonkey-Collage.jpg"><img class="wp-image-1555 aligncenter" src="http://www.ezcare2.com/childcare-blog/wp-content/uploads/PicMonkey-Collage.jpg" alt="" width="758" height="397" /></a><br />
An <a target="_blank" href="http://www.ezcare2.com/childcare-blog/2016/01/5-reasons-why-its-better-to-collect-childcare-payments-via-auto-pay/">auto-pay plan</a><a target="_blank" href="http://www.ezcare2.com/childcare-blog/2016/01/5-reasons-why-its-better-to-collect-childcare-payments-via-auto-pay/"> </a>is the easiest, most efficient way for childcare centers to collect childcare fees and for parents to pay them.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">
<p style="text-align: left;">Literacy is for everyone. <a target="_blank" href="http://blog.brookespublishing.com/literacy-rich-preschool-environments-for-all-learners/">Some centers <span class="il">are</span> harnessing creative ways</a> to ensure that all children have access to books, including children with disabilities.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">
<p style="text-align: left;">Rise and shine! Morning is the most essential time to children transitioning from home to their childcare center or school, so it’s important to <a target="_blank" href="http://www.naeyc.org/yc/files/yc/file/201311/YC1113_Maximze_Influence_Toddler_Mornings_Masterson.pdf">make the most of them</a>.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">
<p style="text-align: left;">Valentine’s Day tends to be packed with cute cards and an abundance of sugary treats. Try out a healthy twist on your Cupid duties this year with <a target="_blank" href="http://www.funkypolkadotgiraffe.net/2013/02/preschool-valentines-youre-my-main.html">easy-to-make, vitamin-rich Valentines</a>.</p>
