                <div class="post-excerpt"><p><a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/category/how-to/" target="_blank"><img class="alignright" style="margin-left: 5px; margin-right: 5px;" title="social media how to" alt="social media how to" width="190" height="166" srcset="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/images/how-to-pose.png, http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/images/how-to-pose@2x.png 2x" /></a> Need some new social media marketing ideas?</p>
<p>Looking for ways to jumpstart your engagement?</p>
<p>Whether your goal is to spread awareness or grow your audience, better engagement is your key to success.</p>
<p>In this article you&#8217;ll <strong>discover three ways to improve engagement for your social media accounts</strong>.</p>
<div style="width: 610px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img alt="social media engagement techniques" width="600" height="315" srcset="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/kl-social-media-engagment-600.png, http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/kl-social-media-engagment-600@2x.png 2x" /><p class="wp-caption-text">Discover three techniques you can use to improve social media engagement.</p></div>
<p><span id="more-97756"></span></p>
<h3>#1:Personalize Your Approach</h3>
<p>Your audience is the greatest asset for your campaign. To run a successful social campaign with plenty of engagement, you have to know your <a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/how-to-research-and-locate-your-audience-using-social-media/" target="_blank">audience</a> and what they&#8217;re likely to respond to and enjoy.</p>
<p>To gain these insights, you&#8217;ll need to do some research. <strong>Find out which social media channels the people you want to reach spend the most time on</strong>.<strong> Discover what topics they care about and what <a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/4-ways-to-improve-engagement-with-hashtags/" target="_blank">hashtags</a> they use.</strong></p>
<p>Don&#8217;t just listen to your audience, but communicate with them. When you <strong>show your fans that you&#8217;re interested and you care</strong>, and they&#8217;ll show you what makes them happy. Once you know what they&#8217;re looking for, you can launch a social media campaign that they&#8217;ll embrace.</p>
<p>Many brands have leveraged Pinterest to run successful social campaigns, including Kotex&#8217;s <a href="http://www.adweek.com/adfreak/kotex-makes-gifts-women-based-their-pinterest-boards-139161" target="_blank">Woman&#8217;s Inspiration Day</a>. The company looked at the Pinterest boards for 50 inspiring women to see what interested them.</p>
<div class="video-container"><span class="youtube"><iframe title="YouTube video player" class="youtube-player" type="text/html" width="480" height="385" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/UVCoM4ao2Tw?wmode=transparent&amp;fs=1&amp;hl=en&amp;modestbranding=1&amp;iv_load_policy=3&amp;showsearch=0&amp;rel=0&amp;theme=dark" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></span></div>
<p>Kotex then brought their inspirations to life by creating handmade gifts and sending them to the women as virtual gifts. If the women then posted something about their virtual gift, they received a real gift from the companyin the mail.</p>
<p>Almost all of the 50 women responded, generating 2,284 interactions and 694,853 impressions on Pinterest. The campaign was a huge hit because Kotex was able to <strong>hyper-target a specific audience and what they want</strong>. This is just one of many examples of how you can use <a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/social-growth-how-to-use-pinterest-to-grow-your-following-on-facebook-youtube-and-beyond-natalie-jill/" target="_blank">Pinterest</a> to launch a successful social campaign.</p>
<h3>#2:Put the Product in Your Fan&#8217;s Hands</h3>
<p>Remember the core goal of social media is to be social, so it&#8217;s important to <strong>cultivate relationships with your fans and followers</strong>. This is an essential step to launching a successful social media campaign.</p>
<p>Yes, your ultimate goal is to <a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/4-tools-to-simplify-social-selling/" target="_blank">sell</a> your product and your brand, but for the purposes of your social media campaign, <strong>take a step back on the selling and simply share</strong>. Don&#8217;t force your product or your brand on your audience or they&#8217;ll stop listening to you. Opt for subtlety instead. <strong>Give people a way to share your product in a fun and unique way</strong> and <strong>let your audience come to it</strong>.</p>
<p>Ford&#8217;s Fiestagram Instagram campaign created buzz for the release of their new Fiesta model by asking fans to post pictures that related to different campaign hashtags.</p>
<!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
<div style="float:none;margin:0px;">
<a href="http://bit.ly/1hwrKIY" target="_blank"><img style="width: 100%;"  src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/SME-Large-Surfer-560x467.jpg" /></a>
</div>

<p>Each hashtag was a buzzword describing one of the features of the new Fiesta car, such as #music, #entry, #hidden, etc. Ford then chose the best pictures for each hashtag and displayed them on digital billboards. They also awarded weekly prizes to fans who submitted photos for the campaign.</p>
<p>More than <a href="http://mashable.com/2012/02/02/ford-fiesta-instagram/#QtfBMHKdrsqw" target="_blank">16,000</a> photos were submitted to the Fiestagram campaign and Ford gained 120,000 new fans on their social media pages.</p>
<p>Lay&#8217;s launched the clever Do Us a Flavor campaign on Facebookthatasked fans and consumers to come up with a new flavor of potato chip for the company to create and sell.Thatyear, nearly 4 million people submitted their flavor ideas via Facebook or text message.</p>
<div style="width: 490px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/lays-entry-call.png" alt="lays contest" width="480" /><p class="wp-caption-text">Lay&#8217;s Do Us a Flavor campaign asked fans to submit ideas for a new flavor of potato chip.</p></div>
<p>Once the finalists were chosen, Lay&#8217;s asked fans to vote for the winning flavor on Facebook, receiving over 1 million votes.The prize for the winning flavor was $1 million (or 1% of sales for the chip flavor).The first campaign increased Lay&#8217;s sales by <a href="https://blog.instant.ly/blog/2014/08/lays-2013-do-us-a-flavor-contest-value-intelligent-concept-testing/" target="_blank">12%</a>and was so successful atgenerating engagementfor the brandthat they continue to run it each year.</p>
<p>If you <strong>offer your fans a chance to showcase their creativity and connect it to one of your products</strong>, you can establish a respectful relationship with your audience, linking their personal creations to your company.</p>
<h3>#3:Play the Tag Game</h3>
<p>Before you launch a <a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/successful-social-media-campaigns/" target="_blank">social media campaign</a>, you need to know what your goal is. Without a firm plan in mind, you&#8217;re almost certainly setting yourself up for failure. It&#8217;s important to <strong>identify not only what you want to accomplish, but also theaudience you&#8217;re targeting and how you want to connect with them</strong>.</p>
<p>The <a href="http://www.alsa.org/fight-als/ice-bucket-challenge.html" target="_blank">ALS Ice Bucket Challenge</a> campaign was launched to raise awareness and funding for ALS. The &#8220;challenge&#8221; was for people to dump a bucket of ice over their heads and record a video of it to increase awareness of the disease.</p>
<div style="width: 510px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/ll-als-post.png" alt="als icebucketchallenge video post" width="500" /><p class="wp-caption-text">People who took the challenge spurred more engagement by nominating others to take the challenge.</p></div>
<p>After each challenge, the person taking it would nominate two or three more people to take the challenge and so on until people around the world were participating. The campaign grew exponentially in a short amount of time. So what made this campaign great? The call to action: Dump ice water on your head, and tag someone else to participate. It was simple, but impactful.</p>
<p>In your social campaigns,<strong> usea simple call to actionto make it easy for people to complete the goal</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Over to You</strong></p>
<p>Social media is one of the greatest tools you can use to grow your business and promote your brand. Launching social media campaigns helps you spread <a href="http://www.socialmediaexaminer.com/how-to-increase-awareness-generate-leads-and-create-advocacy-with-social-media/" target="_blank">awareness</a> and build an audience.</p>
<p>To jumpstart participation in your social media campaign, you need a core group of people who are 100% behind your launch to get it off the ground. One way to do that is to create a beta group and give them exclusive perks.</p>
