<p><span>A new Netflix animated series for children titled&nbsp;</span><em>Beat Bugs</em><span>&nbsp;will feature major names singing some of the&nbsp;</span><a href="http://radio.com/tag/beatles/">Beatles</a><span>&#8216; most beloved songs. Bands and artists who will contribute covers include&nbsp;</span><a href="/tag/Eddie-Vedder" target="_blank">Eddie Vedder</a><span>,&nbsp;</span><a href="/tag/sia" target="_blank">Sia</a><span>, P!nk, James Bay, The Shins, Of Monsters and Men, Chris Cornell, Regina&nbsp;Spektor and&nbsp;more.</span></p>

<p>Debuting in August 2016, the series will use the Beatles&#8217; music to send encouraging messages to viewers. The show reached a deal with&nbsp;Sony/ATV Music Publishing, which allows them to use recorded covers from songs in the &#8220;Northern Songs&#8221;&nbsp;catalog, once owned by Michael Jackson&nbsp;(via&nbsp;<a href="http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/6874141/netflix-animated-series-beatles-songs-covers"><em>Billboard</em></a>).</p>
<p>Some of the covers include&nbsp;&ldquo;Help!,&#8221; &#8220;All You Need Is Love,&#8221; &#8220;Come Together,&#8221; &#8220;Penny Lane,&#8221; &#8220;Yellow Submarine,&#8221; &#8216;&ldquo;Lucy In the Sky With Diamonds,&#8221; &#8220;Sgt. Pepper&rsquo;s Lonely Hearts Club Band,&#8221; and &#8220;Magical Mystery Tour,&#8221; reports&nbsp;<em><a href="http://deadline.com/2016/02/beatles-animated-musical-kids-series-beat-bugs-netflix-beatles-songs-1201699735/" target="_blank">Deadline</a></em>.</p>
<p>Josh Wakely of Grace Films will write, direct and produce the project. Wakely previously did a musical short titled&nbsp;<em>My Mind&#8217;s Own Melody</em>&nbsp;in 2012. Watch the Beat Bugs trailer below.</p>
