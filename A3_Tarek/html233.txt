        <heading id="article-heading">
	        <h1>Watch: Donald Trump is Johnny Depp&#39;s Most Insane Role of All Time</h1>
        </heading>
        
        <article id="main-article" data-module-id="00000152-cba7-d79d-a173-fbafa4670000">
        	<p class="clearfix" id="article-byline">
        			<a href="http://www.indiewire.com/author/zack-sharf"><img class="author-avatar" src="http://dl9fvu4r30qs1.cloudfront.net/a1/6a/3ce82aef4eb1872cf080c3b4f47d/zack-sharf.jpg" alt="Photo of Zack Sharf" />
	        				</a><span class="extra-bold">
        				By 
        				<a href="http://www.indiewire.com/author/zack-sharf">Zack Sharf</a>
        				|
        				Indiewire</span>
					<span>February 10, 2016 at 10:19AM</span>
        		</p>
        	<div id="article-promo">
	        	You've never seen Depp like this before, and that's saying something.</div>
	        
	        <div id="article-social-top">
	        	<span class="share circle-container facebook fa fa-facebook"></span>
	        	<div class="share facebook-share-count">
	        	     <div class="cap-border"></div>

	        	</div>
				<a class="twitter" href="http://twitter.com/share" target="_blank"><span class="share circle-container fa fa-twitter"></span></a>
			<a class="email-share-link" target="_blank" href="http://api.addthis.com/oexchange/0.8/forward/email/offer?url="><span class="share circle-container email fa fa-envelope"></span></a>
	        	<a id="main-comment-proof" href="#comments" class="circle-container comment-proof">1</a>
	        </div>
			<div id="main-content-id" style="display: none;">00000152-cba7-d79d-a173-fbafa4670000</div>
 			<style type="text/css">.cms-textAlign-left{text-align:left;}.cms-textAlign-center{text-align:center;}.cms-textAlign-right{text-align:right;}</style><div class="photo ">
    <div class="img"><!-- size is: poster-large -->
    	<img src="http://dl9fvu4r30qs1.cloudfront.net/76/83/232a56744af88de419d6fd83a4d8/the-art-of-the-deal-the-movie.jpg" width="1504" height="2530" alt="&quot;The Art of the Deal: The Movie&quot;"></div>

    <div class="caption">
            <span class="credit">Funny Or Die</span>
            <span class="text">"The Art of the Deal: The Movie"</span>
        </div>

<p>Forget Johnny Depp's supposed comeback by playing the gangster Whitey Bulger in last year's &quot;Black Mass,&quot; the actor is really proving his chops as Donald Trump in the just-released <a class="" href="http://www.funnyordie.com/videos/ad38087bac/donald-trump-art-of-the-deal-movie?_cc=__d___&amp;_ccid=0eae29929dd9b0ba" target="_blank" title="Link: http://www.funnyordie.com/videos/ad38087bac/donald-trump-art-of-the-deal-movie?_cc=__d___&amp;_ccid=0eae29929dd9b0ba">Funny or Die</a> mockumentary &quot;The Art of the Deal: The Movie.&quot; Yes, you read that correctly, and given the actor's knack for playing bizarre, otherworldly characters, it's rather shocking it took him this long to sink his teeth into Trump.</p><p><a class="" href="http://www.indiewire.com/article/telluride-review-johnny-depp-is-trying-something-very-different-in-black-mass-20150907" target="_blank" title="Link: http://www.indiewire.com/article/telluride-review-johnny-depp-is-trying-something-very-different-in-black-mass-20150907">READ MORE:&nbsp;Telluride Review: Johnny Depp is Trying Something Very Different in 'Black Mass</a></p><p>&quot;The Art of the Deal: The Movie,&quot; which takes its name from Trump’s 1987 bestselling business book, presents itself as a long-lost film Trump made back in the 1980s, now unearthed by one Ron Howard, who serves as the documentary's narrator. Other celebrities who appear include&nbsp;Alfred Molina,&nbsp;Kristen Schaal, Andy Richter, Paul Scheer,&nbsp;&nbsp;Robert Morse, Patton Oswalt, Jack McBrayer, Michaela Watkins, Henry Winkler, Christopher Lloyd, Stephen Merchant and Rob Huebel.</p><p>The project was shot in secret last fall by Funny Or Die Editor-in-Chief Owen Burke, long before anyone involved knew that Trump would still be the frontrunner in the Republican presidential race.</p><div class="photo ">
    <div class="img"><!-- size is: article-large -->
    	<img src="http://cdn.indiewire.psdops.com/dims4/INDIEWIRE/ce9c075/2147483647/thumbnail/680x478/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fdl9fvu4r30qs1.cloudfront.net%2F3c%2F36%2Fac38ee4a454bae1a45ada5106987%2Ftrump-depp.jpg" width="680" height="478" alt="&quot;The Art of the Deal: The Movie&quot;" data-size="article-large"></div>

    <div class="caption">
            <span class="credit">Funny or Die</span>
            <span class="text">"The Art of the Deal: The Movie"</span>
        </div>
    </div>
<p>&quot;The plan was to move really fast because we thought Trump would go away, as least as a presidential candidate,&quot; he told <a class="" href="http://www.nytimes.com/2016/02/11/movies/trump-movie-funny-or-die-johnny-depp.html?partner=rss&amp;emc=rss&amp;_r=1" target="_blank" title="Link: http://www.nytimes.com/2016/02/11/movies/trump-movie-funny-or-die-johnny-depp.html?partner=rss&amp;emc=rss&amp;_r=1">The New York Times</a>&nbsp;about the project. &quot;When he bizarrely didn’t go away, we had a little more time. But that meant keeping the secret for longer.&quot;</p><p>&quot;It was a crazy, completely nuts idea that somehow we pulled off,&quot; added Adam McKay, co-founder of Funny Or Die and current Best Director Oscar nominee for &quot;The Big Short.&quot;</p><p>The performance Depp gives as Trump ranks as some of his most bonkers work ever, which is saying something, given his recent roles in such fare as &quot;Tusk&quot; and&nbsp;&quot;Mortdecai.&quot; The entire 50-minute mockumentary is now available for a limited time <a class="" href="http://www.funnyordie.com/videos/ad38087bac/donald-trump-art-of-the-deal-movie?_cc=__d___&amp;_ccid=0eae29929dd9b0ba" target="_blank" title="Link: http://www.funnyordie.com/videos/ad38087bac/donald-trump-art-of-the-deal-movie?_cc=__d___&amp;_ccid=0eae29929dd9b0ba">exclusively on Funny Or Die</a>, but you can check out some of the absurdity in the hilarious trailer below.</p><iframe src="//www.funnyordie.com/embed/ad38087bac" width="640" height="400" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen webkitallowfullscreen mozallowfullscreen></iframe><div style="text-align:left;font-size:x-small;margin-top:0;width:640px;"><a href="http://www.funnyordie.com/videos/ad38087bac/donald-trump-art-of-the-deal-movie" title="from Funny Or Die, #FODTrumpMovie">Funny Or Die Presents Donald Trump&#39;s The Art Of The Deal: The Movie</a> from <a href="http://www.funnyordie.com/funnyordie">Funny Or Die</a></div><p><br /></p><!-- We dont want to display pagination controls when there is only one page -->
