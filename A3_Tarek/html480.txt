                        <h1 class="the-title">Be Outrageous in Your Desiring!</h1>
                            <div class="cf post-meta-container">
                                <p class="post-meta"><a href="http://www.thefeelgoodshift.com/author/realitymanifestgmail-com/" title="View all posts by Erika Matos" rel="author">Erika Matos</a><a href="http://www.thefeelgoodshift.com/be-outrageous-in-your-desiring/#respond">0 Comments</a></p>                                <p class="post-meta date-extra">January 11 2015</p>

                            							<div class="op-share-code"><span class="st_twitter_hcount" displayText="Tweet"></span><span class="st_plusone_hcount"></span><span class="st_fblike_hcount"></span></div>                            <div class="single-post-content cf">
                                <p><strong>&#8220;I give thanks that I now receive the righteous desires of my heart. Mountains are removed, valleys exalted and every crooked place made straight.  I am in the Kingdom of fulfillment.&#8221;</strong> &#8211; Florence Scovel Shinn</p>
<p><img title="" class=" size-full wp-image-243 aligncenter" src="http://www.thefeelgoodshift.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/outrageous-in-desiring-e1420936342320.png" alt="outrageous in desiring" width="400" height="335" /></p>
<p>Here&#8217;s the only thing you are responsible for: <strong>Identifying what you want.</strong> NOT how it&#8217;ll come to you.</p>
<p>This is one of my favorite intention exercises based on the teachings of Abraham Hicks:</p>
<p><strong>Daily Deliberate Creation Workshop:</strong> Pen and paper needed.</p>
<p>1. Make a list of  wants and<strong> &#8221;Why I Want This.&#8221;</strong></p>
<p>2. On the other side, make a list of <strong>&#8221;Why I KNOW that I Will Create This.&#8221;</strong> to add emotion to the thought of what you want, and building expectation of receiving it.</p>
<p>3.  Ask yourselves, <strong>&#8221;What would it be like, if/when. . .?&#8221;</strong> And let your imagination loose like a pack of wild wolves and dive deep into the deliciousness of that experience.</p>
<p>4. <strong>Create a Prosperity Box</strong> (as fancy-schmanzy as you&#8217;d like) and put the powerfully charged intention (piece of paper) into the box.</p>
<p>5. <strong>Wait for it to manifest.</strong></p>
<p>Once you have completed the above steps, you would have &#8216;launched the creation process!</p>
<p><strong>BONUS:</strong> Declare what you would like to have happen during the next &#8221;segment&#8221; of your day. As often as you need during the day, set an intention for what it is you want to bring into your experience as the day unfolds. <strong>(Builds and AWESOME manifesting muscle!)</strong></p>
<p><strong>Your Turn:</strong> <em>What are some of your favorite manifesting techniques and tools?</em></p>
<p>Share this with you friends too! I&#8217;d love to know what they have in their manifesting toolbox.</p>
<p>To your Outrageous Wanting,</p>
<p>Erika</p>
