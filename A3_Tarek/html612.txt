<p class="releaseDateline">New York, NY (PRWEB) January 29, 2016 </p><p> On Tuesday, March 1 Cynopsis will host the interactive webinar “<a href="http://www.cynopsis.com/webinar/new-social-media-strategies-reach-young-demo/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">New Social Media Strategies to Reach the Young Demos</a>.” Learn how to leverage social media platforms like Wishbone and Whisper, as well as “old” standbys Twitter, Facebook, Instagram and Snapchat, for maximum impact.</p>
<p>Cynopsis Editorial Director Lynn Leahey will host the March 1 webinar along with industry experts including Ed Haslam, CMO of ShareThis, Jamie Stewart, Media Director for redpepper and Suzanne Park, VP of Marketing for AMC, who will advise on the latest tools you need to engage young viewers and drive tune-in.</p>
<p>This webinar offers a deep dive into:
<br /></p><ul class="releaseul"><li>  How to engage with Gen Y and Gen Z on their must-have apps</li>
 <li> The key to mapping out a social media strategy for an earned media result</li>
 <li> Using social to build time-shifted audience</li>
 <li> The importance of audience empowerment</li>
 <li> How to use social to maximize value from your content</li>
 <li> The relationship between TV chatter and tune-in</li></ul><p></p>
<p>This highly interactive webinar will allow for real time Q&amp;A’s to ensure your specific questions are answered. Register today for the Cynopsis webinar  “<a href="http://www.cynopsis.com/webinar/new-social-media-strategies-reach-young-demo/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">New Social Media Strategies to Reach the Young Demos</a>.”</p>
<p>All webinar attendees will receive full access to speaker presentations for 1 year. Webinar tuition is based on location, not number of participants, so each registration site can have multiple participants for one low price. Questions? Contact Trish Pihonak at trish(at)cynopsis(dot)com.</p>
<p>About Cynopsis Media:
<br />Cynopsis Media is what the TV industry reads first every day. The Cynopsis Media family of products includes the media industry&#39;s most-read daily, Cynopsis, plus sister dailies Cynopsis Digital and Cynopsis Sports, the Cynopsis Jobs board and special reports. Serving TV, agency and brand professionals, Cynopsis Media produces conferences, webinars, and awards programs that are second to none. Find out more at cynopsis.com. </p>
