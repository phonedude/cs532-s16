Super Bowl 50 ESPN Picks: Experts way off before season

by Michael Pallas 1w ago Follow @michael_pallas

  • TWEET
  • SHARE
  • GPLUS
  • Fancred
  • 0 Comments

We all have our Super Bowl 50 predictions.  But don’t worry if you or your friends are completely wrong since ESPN was too before the season even started.

Before the season, ESPN experts made their selections.  The panel consisted of 48 people.  They were former NFL players, former NFL Front office members, NFL insiders, Frank Caliendo and Chris Berman.

Stay connected with The Jet Press

Follow @TheJetPress
