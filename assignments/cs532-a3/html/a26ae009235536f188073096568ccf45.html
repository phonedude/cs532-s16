Super Bowl 50 halftime show is as important as the game itself. Super Bowl 50  will officially start on February 7 and Super Bowl Sunday is always a historic one.

But, have you wondered what Americans usually eat during Super Bowl Sunday?

Dubbed as Golden Super Bowl, this year’s NFL Season 50 will see a much-awaited battle between Denver Broncos and Carolina Panthers. For sure, it will be a festive time with foods on the table. And guess what are the top 7 foods that Americans love to eat during Super Bowl party?

Take a look!

Top 7 Staple Foods During Super Bowl Sunday

  • Over 1.3 Billion chicken wings in one day!
  • Over 2 Million pizzas sold, for Pizza Hut alone!
  • Over 5,000 lbs of hotdogs sold during the actual game
  • Over 49.2 Million cases of beer sold
  • Over 14,500 lbs of chips
  • Over 8,000 lbs of guacamole dip
  • Over 4,000 tons of popcorn

Read more: Super Bowl 50: 5 Healthy And Relatively Inexpensive Food Ideas For Super Bowl Halftime

Just by looking at this list, it is easy to imagine the negative effects it can do to your health. Talk about processed meat, saturated and trans fat, cholesterol. All for the love of the game!

Did you know that such intense games can cause heart attack? And you throw in these kind of foods in one day, heart problems will surely happen.

High blood pressure is a telling sign of heart problem. And Super Bowl Sunday is bound increase fans’ blood pressure. They can feel irritable, easily excited, easily afraid, and easily angered. The excitement of the game can affect fans’ health.

Then you have show-stopping performances during the famed Super Bowl halftime show.  Some celebrities even make major announcements during their performances. Definitely, people at the venue and those watching at home will be at the edge of their seats.

While it can be easy to get lost in the emotions of the game and indulge in the moment, it will be wise to keep watch of your health and enjoy the game without ending in the hospital.

Have a happy and healthy Super Bowl Sunday!
