Jack Savoretti NFL Q&A

Posted Nov 3, 2015

  • a
  • a

Jack Savoretti is the latest artist to take part in our Eight of the Best NFL Q&A.

We continue our weekly musical interview series with another star from the world of pop music. This week we speak to Jack Savoretti:

1.  How did you become an NFL fan? I grew up in the time of the New York Giants and the Miami Dolphins. I remember going to New York for the first time as a kid and just going nuts in a sport shop and wanting everything that had anything to do with the Giants.

2. If you could play any professional sport, what sport would you play and why? I actually wanted to be a football (soccer) player as a kid … im half english and half Italian with a tyouch of german … football is in my blood!

3. Who is the most rock & roll player? (past or present) Bo Jackson … He is the only athlete to be named an all star in two major American sports

4. What song would you like to be played as your team takes the field? Pavartotti - “Nessun Dorma”

5. In what US football stadium would you most like to play a gig? Levis Stadium San Francisco

6. What is your all-time greatest sports movie? Rocky…

7. Who would be your ultimate fantasy quarterback? Dan Marino

8. Finally, who’s going to lift the Superbowl Trophy this season? Im gonna say the Seatle Seahawks

Jacks album ‘Written In The Scars’ is out now http://www.jacksavoretti.com
