Ep 353: Boycotts & Censorship & Trump, oh my!

February 1st, 2016

Marc, Bryon and Rocky talk about the boycotting of the Oscars, Marvel donates to Trump, who could voice Darth Vader in the future and Enfield makes national news (again).

Topics & Spoilers:
Star Wars News, Duct Tape Challenge, Marvel, DC, Donald Trump, The Oscars, Harley Quinn up for a GLAAD Award, The Outsiders and Censorship.
