      Coldplay prepares to hit the Super Bowl stage

      January 31, 2016, 11:28 AM|When the discussion of best rock band comes up, Coldplay is normally in the conversation. The British group's been riding a wave of success since releasing their hit song, "Clocks," back in 2002. Now, nearly 14 years later, Coldplay is poised to play before their largest audience ever, when they headline the Super Bowl 50 halftime show. Anthony Mason talks to frontman Chris Martin, guitarist Jon Buckland, drummer Will Champion and bass player Guy Berryman about the biggest gig of their career.
