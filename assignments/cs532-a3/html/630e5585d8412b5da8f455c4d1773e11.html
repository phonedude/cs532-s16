Broccoli and Cauliflower Salad Recipe

Are you having a party, a family event or just wondering what to bring to a potluck? Try making this easy, delicious and healthy Broccoli and Cauliflower Salad Recipe. Your family and friends will love it! Make sure you bring the recipe with you, because you are sure to be asked how to make this delicious salad.

Ingredients

Salad

1 head of broccoli cut into small florets
1 head of cauliflower cut into small florets
1 small red onion finely diced
1 cup of dried raisins
1 cup of sunflower seeds
1 cup of real bacon bits

Dressing

1 cup of mayonnaise
2 tablespoons of white vinegar
2 tablespoons of sugar

In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients for the salad.
Mix together the ingredients for the dressing and poor over the salad.
Toss until blended.
Refrigerate for about 30 minutes or more before serving.

Broccoli and Cauliflower Salad Recipe

Rating: 51

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

    Salad
  • 1 head of broccoli cut into small florets
  • 1 head of cauliflower cut into small florets
  • 1 small red onion finely diced
  • 1 cup of dried raisins
  • 1 cup of sunflower seeds
  • 1 cup of real bacon bits
    • Dressing
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients for the salad.
  2. Mix together the ingredients for the dressing and poor over the salad.
  3. Toss until blended.
  4. Refrigerate for about 30 minutes or more before serving.
