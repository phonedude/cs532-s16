Jeb Bush, Peyton Manning back one another

By The Associated Press
Posted:   02/06/2016 07:59:22 PM MST
Updated:   02/06/2016 08:17:57 PM MST

Left, Republican presidential candidate, former Florida Gov. Jeb Bush answers a question during a Republican presidential primary debate hosted by ABC News at the St. Anselm College Saturday, Feb. 6, 2016, in Manchester, N.H. Right, Denver Broncos quarterback Peyton Manning speaks to reporters in Santa Clara, Calif., Tuesday, Feb. 2, 2016. (Associated Press Photos)

BEDFORD, N.H. — Republican presidential candidate Jeb Bush is backing one of his campaign donors in Sunday's Super Bowl.

Bush said Saturday he's rooting for Denver Broncos quarterback Peyton Manning, "because he's for me."

Manning has contributed the maximum $2,700 to Bush's presidential campaign.

The Broncos face the Carolina Panthers in Sunday's Super Bowl. That's two days before voters go to the polls in New Hampshire.
