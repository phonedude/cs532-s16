What exactly is the Super Bowl and where can I see it in Cardiff?

  • 17:47, 27 Jan 2016
  • Updated 17:50, 27 Jan 2016
  • By Lorna Doran

You don't have to leave the capital to enjoy one of the world's biggest sporting occasions

  •   Shares
  •   Comments

If you can't make it to California this year to watch the Super Bowl fear not, you can watch all the action in Cardiff.

Here's everything you need to know about one of the biggest sporting events in the US calendar and where you can watch it in the Welsh capital...

What is the Super Bowl?

The Super Bowl is the annual championship game of the National Football League (NFL), the highest level of professional football in the US.

Where and when is it?

In 2016 it will be the 50th edition of the Super Bowl and it will be held at the Leviâs Stadium in Santa Clara, California, home of the San Francisco 49ers, on February 7.

Poll loading …

Which teams are playing?

The 50th Super Bowl is between the Carolina Panthers and the Denver Broncos

When is it on in the UK?

In the UK, Panthers v Broncos kicks off at 11.30pm on Sunday February 7

Where can I see it in Cardiff?

WALKABOUT

Head to Walkabout, the Australian chain bar popular for its big-screen sports coverage:

Walkabout

65-74 St Mary St

Cardiff

CF10 1FA

What can I expect to see?

A great viewing experience with big screens and full live HD commentary in a party atmosphere.

Watch the build-up from around 10pm with Walkaboutâs real time statistics, team profiles and live social media feeds shown alongside highlights from the NFL on the screens.

The venue will be filled with all things NFL, including the food, drinks and decor.

You'll also get to see the half-time entertainment, which this year features British band Coldplay.

Can I order food?

You can pre-order a party package that includes a hot dog meal, three chicken wings and two bottles of Budweiser for only £12.

Do I need a ticket?

No, but to guarantee a good view of the screen and table service it's best to book on www.walkaboutbars.co.uk/SuperBowl

Here's where else in Cardiff you can see the Super Bowl... and if you know of somewhere not mentioned pop it in the comments box below:

SHOOTERS SPORTS BAR

Watch the game on a HD screen that's so big you'll think you're on the pitch.

The Cardiff venue is taking bookings for the event for groups of all sizes. Just give them a call on 08455 333 000 or fill in their online booking enquiry form.

Shooters

Stadium Plaza

Wood Street

Cardiff

CF10 1LA

The standard menu has burgers, hot dogs, pizzas, ribs, chicken, salads and sharing platters as well as classics like chilli, pie, lasagne and wraps.

Shooters also offers a number of American-style VIP packages. The £10-a-head bronze package gives you a free shot, hot dog or burger, VIP seating in front of a big screen and dedicated waitress service.

Upgrade to the £15-a-head silver package which includes two bottles of Budweiser, or the gold package which costs £20 a head and includes everything you'd get in both bronze and silver plus an additional Shooters Food Platter.

THE BUNKER

Make it a night to remember and book a booth for you and your friends at the Bunker Rock Lounge & Sports Bar.

You'll need to buy a ticket which costs £60 for a booth or £3 for a standard entry ticket.

There are food and drink deals available, too, so you can eat, drink and be merry while cheering on your favourite team.

It's open from 9pm on the night to 4am on the Monday.

The Bunker

60-61 St Mary Street

Cardiff

CF10 1FE
