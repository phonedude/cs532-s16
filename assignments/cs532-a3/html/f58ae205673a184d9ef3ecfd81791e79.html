      New Orleans Pelicans vs. Los Angeles Lakers, live updates and fan chat

      Kobe Bryant
      Los Angeles Lakers guard Kobe Bryant stands on the court during a review in the fourth quarter of an NBA basketball game against the Denver Nuggets, Tuesday, Dec. 30, 2014, in Denver. Bryant was given three free throw shots on the play after review and the Lakers held off the Nuggets to post a 111-103 victory. (AP Photo/David Zalubowski) ORG XMIT: CODZ120 (David Zalubowski)
      Print Email
      John Reid, NOLA.com | The Times Picayune By John Reid, NOLA.com | The Times Picayune The Times-Picayune
      Email the author | Follow on Twitter
      on February 04, 2016 at 8:00 PM, updated February 08, 2016 at 1:46 PM
      comments

      The New Orleans Pelicans are coming off a 110-97 loss to the San Antonio Spurs on Thursday night at the AT & T Center. Now, the Pelicans play a Lakers team that is 4-23 on the road.

      But the Lakers have guard Kobe Bryant, who will retire after this season. Bryant was listed as questionable to play with a sore right shoulder. He will return to play his final game at the Smoothie King Center on April 8.

      Pelicans point guard Tyreke Evans is listed as doubtful after missing four consecutive games with tendinitis in his right knee. In their loss to the Spurs, the Pelicans committed 19 turnovers and were held to 18 points in the fourth quarter.
