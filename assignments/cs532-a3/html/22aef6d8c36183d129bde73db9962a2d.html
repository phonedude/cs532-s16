I continued to free fall towards this, as i was falling towards it, it now looked like a red set of theater curtains in a complete circle, they were drawn shut. Was this my return from somewhere, was there a message, a secret to be unlocked. I am now aware of how aware i was as a child, Today I am 30 years old, this week I made a cloud vanish with my mind.
