7 Places to Watch the Super Bowl in Orange County

Enjoy game-time appetizers like customizable meat balls at Stag Bar—one of the best spots to watch the Super Bowl in Orange County.

Raise a glass to game day specials, half-time fun and big screen televisions. We’ve rounded up seven of our favorite spots to watch the Super Bowl in Orange County on Feb. 7.

Stag Bar
Surround yourself with 16 big-screen televisions, drinks and delicious game-day eats for Super Bowl 50. The newly re-opened Stag Bar in Newport Beach offers perfect Super Bowl pairings such as  sweet chili, red curry, buffalo, or hot wings, made-to-order varieties of meatballs with customized sauces and cheeses and creatively named, hand-tossed gourmet pizzas such as meat coma or hot and bothered. Plus, choose from new signature cocktails, craft beers and 75 small batch whiskeys. 121 McFadden Place, Newport Beach, 949.673.4470. stagbar.com

 

The Hub Kitchen & Taps
Stop by The Hub Kitchen & Taps this week to choose your square on the Super Bowl square board—free with the purchase an entree and a beer. Come back on Super Bowl Sunday for a pre-game brunch party and the chance to win prizes like a $100 gift card to The Hub. Game day specials include $3 Bud Lights and $15 buckets of Golden Road Point the Way IPA. 1749 Newport Blvd., Costa Mesa, 949.356.5544. thehubcm.com

 

Caffé Primo
Form your own team this Super Bowl Sunday for beer pong matches and flip cup tournaments at Caffé Primo. In addition to food and drink specials such as discounted pitchers, there will be raffles, giveaways and more. 195 W. Center Street Promenade, Anaheim, 714.490.0306. iloveprimo.com

 

Cruisers
Surround yourself with 25 televisions, including a 170-inch mega screen, in four different viewing areas this Super Bowl Sunday. Enjoy happy hour specials and game day classics like nachos, pizzas, sliders, wings and more. 801 E. Balboa Blvd., Newport Beach, 949.220.9414.  cruiserspizzabargrill.com

 

The Blind Pig
Raise your glass for your team as The Blind Pig will have special game day cocktails crafted specifically for the Broncos and the Panthers. Enjoy discounted brews and special menu items. 31431 Santa Margarita Parkway, Rancho Santa Margarita, 949.888.0072. theblindpigoc.com

 

Holsteins Shakes & Buns
Savor a Super Bowl-themed burger for each team at Holsteins burger face-off. Whether you’re a Broncos or Panthers fan, still upset that your team didn’t make it, or purely interested in the commercials, the game day specials will make your mouth water. The Bronco burger features a Colorado lamb burger topped with fire roasted green chilis, Swiss cheese and tomato cumin aioli. The Carolina BBQ burger includes a beef patty with Carolina-style pulled pork, vinegar slaw and crispy onion straws. $18-$19. South Coast Plaza, 3333 Bristol St., Costa Mesa, 714.429.7640. holsteinsburgers.com

 

Oak Grill
Watch Super Bowl 50 on Oak Grill’s big screen and enjoy a special Super Bowl game day menu with favorites including burgers, wings, shishito peppers and more. Also featured are $5 Drafts, $15 pitchers of beer and $10 Cocktails in honor of each team. Reservations encouraged. Island Hotel, Oak Grill, 690 Newport Center Drive, Newport Beach, 949.760.4920. oakgrillnb.com
