5 things you need to know about #photography for 2/7/16

February 7, 2016 by Tj·0 Comments

Today we keep your drone safe on this Super Bowl Sunday, take a look at a new tool from Adobe and help you develop your daily routine to be more productive. This and more in today’s edition of Photo News Today! 

 

With the Super Bowl later today, make sure you keep your drones at home if you live near the stadium or anywhere in San Fran for that matter. San Fransciso has been deemed a no drone zone for this weekend.

http://www.diyphotography.net/faa-declares-much-of-sf-a-no-drone-zone-for-super-bowl-says-it-could-shoot-down-invasive-drones/

Adobe post is a new tool from Adobe that helps you create cool works of art with your photos by mixing them with different words and sayings. Take a look at the new tool below!

http://blogs.adobe.com/jkost/2016/02/introducing-adobe-post.html

Until recently I was a mess during the day and I could not for the life of me find balance between my full time job, my photography business (my other full time job) and most important my life. I set out to find a system that would help me do that and I found by simply blocking out time in my calendar for these items really helped me. Jasmine has some other tips on improving your daily routine to make your days packed full of productivity.

http://www.jasminestarblog.com/index.cfm?postID=2232&establishing-a-daily-routine-for-entrepreneurs

As we have reported previously, goPro is not doing well financially. Today they announced they will be downsizing to having 3 products in their product line. The Session, The Silver and The Black. More information below.

http://www.diyphotography.net/gopro-downsizing-to-three-models-hero5-release-pushed-back/

The article has since been taken down but recently a bridal magazine had an article on what vendors that brides should feed on the wedding day. Photographers were not on the list. This sparked outrage and forced the magazine to un-publish the article.

http://petapixel.com/2016/02/04/brides-magazine-dont-feed-your-photographers/
