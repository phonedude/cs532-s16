Touchdown with the Rusty Rudder this Super Sunday

Events, Sports
February 4, 2016
by Holy City Sinner

The Carolina Panthers are Super Bowl bound and the Rusty Rudder in Mt. Pleasant wants to celebrate with all of their fans this Sunday. Starting at 4 pm, the restaurant will host an all-inclusive party.

The event will include:

  • Food from an exclusive "Big Game menu"
  • Domestic beer
  • House liquor
  • House wine
  • Live music from The Larry David Project  (4 pm - 6 pm)
  • Multiple TVs (indoors and outdoors)

Tickets are $50 per person and can be purchased in advance at The Rusty Rudder or by calling 843-388-3177.

The Rusty Rudder Mt. Pleasant is the official Roaring Riot Mount Pleasant Carolina Panther’s viewing location.

For more information, visit the Facebook event page.
