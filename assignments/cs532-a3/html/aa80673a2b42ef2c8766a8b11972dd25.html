Continuing the NFL's "gold" theme, San Francisco's Levi's Stadium will be selling a one-of-a-kind item during Super Bowl 50.

A sausage topped with real flakes of gold.

Per Food-Management.com, the Big 5-0 Sausage is made of a custom blend of 50 ingredients and thrown into a poppy seed bun along with sauteed bell peppers and ketchup. Taking things to the next level, flakes of gold are sprinkled on top of the sausage.

Now that's how you turn an ordinary concession stand item into a classy dish for the biggest sporting event in the United States.

According to Mashable's Sarah Spigelman Richter (NSFW), the Big 5-0 Sausage will be available for $12. While that may seem expensive for something from the concession stands, this is the Super Bowl; spending $10-plus on food should be expected.
