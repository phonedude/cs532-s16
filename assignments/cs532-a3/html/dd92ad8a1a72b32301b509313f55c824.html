So, what the hell is a PropTickle anyway?

Good question! PropTickle is the only Fantasy Sports site of its kind. At PropTickle, you pick the outcome a various prop bets (sometimes originated from Vegas, sometimes from the mind of the Brother Matt), or a series of prop bets called a Tickle.  Compete against your friends and strangers for real cash payouts! (This is definitely not gambling).

What is a Prop Bet? A proposition bet (or Prop Bet) denotes a bet made regarding the occurrence or non-occurrence during a game (usually a gambling game) of an event not directly affecting the game’s final outcome. Example: The number of rebounds Roy HIbbert is over or under 3.5 (I’d bet under).

So, What’s a Tickle? A Tickle refers to making an outrageous parlay of bets.  A wise man once said: “If you’re going to parlay your picks, you better tickle it a little.” That man was Brendan Dassey, maybe.

Here at PropTickle, you can play it safe and choose to wager on individual prop bets. Or go big with some Tickles.
