The Walking Dead Zombie Bowl Teaser Released

  • - 02/06/2016
  • 0 comments

Team Humans vs. Team Walkers. Find out who wins during the #TWDZombieBowl this Sunday at 9am|8c. https://t.co/iXrylw1w0z

— The Walking Dead AMC (@WalkingDead_AMC) February 6, 2016

The Walking Dead on AMC is getting in on the Super Bowl fun on Sunday, with a marathon of episodes from the series in what they're marketing "The Zombie Bowl."

With that in mind, they released the "Zombie Bowl" teaser video on twitter promoting the marathon event. They'll be showing every episode of seasons 1 and 2 all day Sunday, so if you're not all about seeing Panthers and Broncos fight, you can watch humans fight zombies instead.

The teaser uses NFL-style music and voiceover in an extra bit of fun. Of course, if you want to do a marathon of your own, every season so far is available on Netflix, too.
