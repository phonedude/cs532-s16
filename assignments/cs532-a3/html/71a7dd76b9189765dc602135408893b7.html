On February 7, Super Bowl 50 between the Carolina Panthers and Denver Broncos will kick off at Levi’s Stadium in Santa Clara, Cali. The Panthers will be looking for their first championship in franchise history while the Broncos hope to secure their third. But we all know the Super Bowl is bigger than just the game. From advertisements to parties, it’s become a pop cultural phenomenon in our society. The Viewer's Guide has it all for every type of fan: food recipes, party tips, games and more. It's the ultimate source for all your Super Bowl 50 needs.
