I started a blog to inform vacationers and locals of tips for living or traveling to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico.  My husband and I fell in love with Cabo over 10 years ago.  We consider Cabo "Paradise!" and we hope you will discover the love we have for this amazing destination also. If you have been once, you will be back. We have experienced so much while in Mexico and wanted to share our insights with others. I hope you will find my blog resourceful. I am continuously updating tips as things change.
