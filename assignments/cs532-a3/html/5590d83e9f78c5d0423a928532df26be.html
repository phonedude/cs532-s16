List of IMAX venues

This is a list of worldwide IMAX venues.

Many of the IMAX theaters are listed as digital 3D, except those noted as 15/70 mm, Laser Imax, or Dual Projection (which have both 15/70 mm and digital capability).

15/70 mm locations use 70 mm film projectors, with the film running through horizonally, each frame being 15 perforations wide. 3D presentations are shown using two projectors and either linear polarized or LCD shutter glasses. Laser locations use two 4K resolution digital projectors with laser light sources, with 3D content using Dolby 3D wavelength multiplex visualization. Both formats are capable of displaying aspect ratios as tall as 1.43:1.

Digital 3D IMAX theaters use two 2K resolution projectors with Xenon light sources, use linear polarized 3D glasses, and are capable of displaying aspect ratios as tall as 1.89:1.
