Super Bowl picks from our writing staff

This Sunday is the biggest day in the NFL no matter what team you root for, it’s Super Bowl Sunday! Now we could reminisce about the ’85 Bears some more (but we save that for the other 364 days of the year), or talk about what the Chicago Bears need to do in 2016. We could breakdown Jay Cutler’s obvious improvement from 2014 to 2015. But, instead we’re just going to focus on the big game this weekend, like everybody else.

Writers for the Chicago Bears and Arizona Cardinals have come together and give their two-cents and who they’re picking for this year’s match-up between the Carolina Panthers and Denver Broncos. And I’ll start us off…

B. Okroi’s Super Bowl pick

Everyone’s riding Carolina Panthers in the National media, and only a handful of people have picked the Broncos. To me it’s obvious why, the Carolina Panthers are the better overall team. Cam Newton is at a whole other level in comparison to Peyton Manning this season, and the defenses and special team play have been comparable to each other.

With that being said, I’m picking the Denver Broncos. Ha. I’ve said it once, I’ll say it again, it’s his year. It’s fate. It’s his 18th season in the NFL and he wears the number 18, so it doesn’t matter if the Panthers are better, the stage has already been set for the greatest quarterback of all time to seal his legacy and ride off into the sunset, like his GM John Elway.

SB Final: Denver Broncos win, 20-27
MVP: Peyton Manning
