What to wear to a Superbowl party

Sunday, February 07, 2016

Although I'm not a huge football fan (don't hate me), I do love getting together with friends for a Superbowl party. If you're heading out tonight to watch the Broncos play the Panthers, here are a few comfy outfit ideas for watching the game in style. Of course a team sweatshirt or tee can replace any of the tops here if you're a die-hard fan!


Top left:  Go super casual with a soft leggings or sweats, a tee, sneakers and jean jacket.
Top right:  Go edgy with  faux leather or waxed skinny jeans or leggings paired with a long sweater and flats or heels. Booties would work for this outfit as well.
Bottom left:  A simple sweater dress is perfect for any occasion including a Superbowl party. Tone things down with tights, riding boots and a scarf.
Bottom right:  Casual chic--jeans, a simple sweater, a long chunky cardigan, and ankle boots make for a cozy and comfy party outfit.

Happy Superbowl--hope the team  you're rooting for wins!
