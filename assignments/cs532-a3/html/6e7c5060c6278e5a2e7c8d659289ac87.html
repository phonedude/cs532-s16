Super Food Ideas For Sunday

Admiral BigGun
Recipes, Tailgating

So there is a game this Sunday, you are having friends over to watch and plan to order a pizza or two.

Dude… seriously, Don’t be that guy.

I know it seams overwhelming… but trust me, you don’t have to be a experienced 5 star grill master to have a great party… let me give you a few ideas to get you going in the right direction.

First, lets start with a few easy ones… Remember: Crock pots are your friend

SIMPLE SIMPLE SIMPLE

Little BigGuns (bacon wrapped smokies in brown sugar… nothing says Super Party like Little BigGuns)

mmmmm covered in bacon and brown sugar…. now, we wait.

Little Smokies (need a last minute horse-diver? grab your favorite BBQ sauce and try this! yeah… i know, my spelling isn’t all that good)

Gameday Slow Cooker BBQ Meatballs (Making barbecue meatballs in a crock pot is one of the staples of game day.)

Easy & yummy game day meatballs!

Jackie’s Famous Meatballs (a strange wonderful mix of two things you probably already have in your house… these appetizers are simply unforgettable)

Crock Pot Chili (very simple and low maintenance way of making sure your guests have plenty of good food, while you watch the game)

Awesome Venison Chili (Have some venison in the freezer? Today is the perfect day to show off what a great hunter you are with this yummy concoction)

Chili Dogs are a great food on game day!

Crock Pot Chili Dogs (as long as you are doing chili, why not go all out and make a chili dog bar with all the fixin’s)

Slow Cooker Potatoes with Chives and Bacon (Keep Calm and Chive on with these simple slow cooker potatoes. KCCO!)

Bacon and Chives make potatoes NOM!

Bacon Deviled Eggs (this one doesn’t even need a crock pot)

Thor’s Caviar (some versions known as Texas Caviar, make sure to have plenty of this on hand!)

If you like Texas Caviar, try this!

 

WINGS WINGS WINGS

Tangy Hot Wings (Love Frank’s hot sauce? You will want to see this one)

Hot wings are great on game day

Honey Mustard Wings (Crock Pot friendly – Admiral BigGun approved!)

Spicy Asian Chicken Wings (I prefer doing these on the grill… you choose what works best for you)

Asian Wing Sauce

Red Hot Honey Wings (Turn up the heat… AND … stay out of the kitchen)

Slow Cooker Root Beer Wings (a favorite at my house, tough to go wrong with this one)

Garlic and Beer Wings (you will need to make a trip to Cabella’s not that THAT should be a problem)

A Football Gameday Without Wings is like Birthday Without Cake

Cheesy Poof Wings (to do this right, you really need to fire up the grill)

SPEAKING OF YOUR GRILL

Tequila Grilled Chicken Legs (SUPERSIZE your wings by making them out of drumsticks!)

Bacon Wrapped Turkey Legs (SUPER-SUPER SIZE your drumsticks and make Ron Swanson proud)

Spicy Sausage and Shrimp Skewers (gotta love food on a stick!)

Sweet Pepper Shrimp and Sausage Kebabs

Smoky Cheddar Jalapeño poppers (made without cream cheese and stuffed with meat)

Pineapple Bacon on a Stick (simple to grill – awesome to eat)

Jalapeño Mushroom Bombs (even people that don’t like things spicy, love these. They have just the right amount of awesome)

Jalapeño Mushroom Bombs with BACON!

I Triple Dog Dare You (his baby is one mega-massive-meat dog made with three (count ’em) three meats and pepper jack cheese.)

Bacon Beer Brats (not as complex as the Tripple Dog Dare you, yet still wonderful… plus BEER!)

Grilled Steak Fajitas (a little more involved, but the payoff is great)

 

TOO SIMPLE?????

Then why not go all out this year and create a Masterpiece?

The Orangewood Smoked PorkLordy Trophy (smoked ham wrapped with bacon and shaped like a SuperBowl trophy… your friends won’t forget your party anytime soon)

Behold the PORK LORDY TROPHY!!!

So now that i have taken away your excuses… what are you making on gameday?
