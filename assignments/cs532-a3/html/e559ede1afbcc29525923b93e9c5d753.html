Where to Watch the Super Bowl in Austin

Eater Staff on Feb 1, 2016, 10:13a

Punch Bowl Social
Punch Bowl Social/Facebook

The true magic of Super Bowl, aside from football, revolves around eating and drinking. If you aren’t hosting or crashing a house party with tasty takeout, Austin’s bars and restaurants are happy to accommodate on Sunday, February 7. From craft cocktails at Whisler’s to free hot dogs at Lala’s to a soup competition at Craftsman, you’ll have a hard time choosing this Super Bowl. If there’s a bumpin’ party missing from this list, let us know in the comments or on the tipline.

—Anastacia Uriegas

1 Whisler's

Whisler’s is throwing a super-fun Super Bowl party. Not only will the game be shown inside and outside, look out for Oasis Brewery beer, Bloody Marys, panther- and horse-inspired cocktails, and food from La Barbecue.

Austin, TX 78702
