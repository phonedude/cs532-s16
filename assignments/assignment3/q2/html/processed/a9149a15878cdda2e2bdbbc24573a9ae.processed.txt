This Exclusive Clip From ‘Infliction’ Will Hit You Like A Brick

Dave Dreher 
07/30/2014
News



To call director Jack Thomas Smith’s latest effort, an assembled footage flick titled ‘Infliction’ disturbing would be like calling water wet.  This is one crazy ass film and quite frankly, I don’t want to ruin the viewing for you by giving you the slightest clue of what you’re in for.
Jack was kind enough to send along an exclusive clip that give you just a little taste of what you can expect.


‘Infliction’ is out now and available in just about any form or format you can imagine. Just click on one of the links below and you could be watching this one in a matter of seconds. 
iTunes – http://bit.ly/1pBlced
Amazon – http://www.amazon.com/Infliction-Artists-Not-Provided/dp/B00JPFTWC8
Walmart – http://www.walmart.com/ip/36977350
Best Buy – http://www.bestbuy.com/site/infliction-dvd/25053103.p?id=3223414&%3BskuId=25053103

Here are the links for Digital downloads and VOD:
Cinema Now – https://www.cinemanow.com/title/596767
Google Play – https://play.google.com/store/movies/details/Infliction?id=SmLnP-14TUQ&hl=en
Vimeo – http://vimeo.com/ondemand/infliction
VUDU – http://www.vudu.com/movies/#!content/542110/Infliction
Here’s what Jack has had to say about his experience on this project:
“Working on ‘Infliction’ left me troubled and haunted, It left me thinking about people’s actions or lack thereof and the inevitable domino effect. We all walk our own path in life, which shapes and defines us. What happens to us today, good or bad, will affect generations to come.”
Like I said above – it’s available now so go nab it and give it a view.
