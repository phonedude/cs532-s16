Stuck in the boat?

February 12, 2013 Farmer Phil 7 Comments



Yesterday as I was driving into work and praying the prayer of having courage to step out of the boat came to my mind. I was thinking about the faith it took Paul to trust Jesus for who He was and to step out and walk on water. 
Questions like: Where are you at? How much will you trust Him? Are you willing to step out from the comfort? Is your faith big enough to step out of the boat? 
Next thing I see a Tweet about the 40 day devotional that our church is doing with the heading: Day 13: It is easier to believe when you’re in the boat. Wow, was that timely! As I read it this morning as the devotionals tend to be sent after my devotional time, wow did it hit home.

It’s easier to believe and proclaim faith when you are in the boat, talk is cheap.
When we take our focus off Jesus and let the burdens or problems around us become bigger than the One who can save us, we lose hope too.
The redemptive work of God is just like Jesus’ outstretched hand and He catches us in the moment.
He reminds us that there is no need to doubt, we are secure in Him.
In these moments we speak the words that the disciples spoke as well, affirming our faith once again, “Truly you are the Son of God.” 

As I look ahead I pray that my eyes will stay focused on Jesus and even in that my morning started out rough with high stress and anxiety. But God’s Word says be anxious for nothing, but in all things through prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be known, so I tuned into Spirit1053.com to listen to some encouraging music and praising God while I worked and prayed and found a peace that goes beyond understanding.
So is it all talk while sitting in the boat? Is it a leap of faith as you step out and show how much you trust God? Where are you at today? What are you going to do? Don’t be motivated out of fear for that is what the devil desires and is trying to create and cause. Be motivated out of love and faith to pursue after God’s calling and plan for your life.
So today, let’s have undaunted courage and faith because we know where our help comes from. 
Today, I want to be like Peter…

“Lord, if it’s you,” Peter replied, “tell me to come to you on the water.” – Matthew 14:28-29

Lord, if it’s you, tell me to come out of my boat and step onto the water… For I want to come and walk with you in a new way with focus on You and Your will, without fear, and with undaunted courage.
