New challenge for 2006 World Cup winner Del Piero…

Friday 19/09/2014 10:01 
Comments: 








...





...





...



 
 
Foto: Getty Images







...





...





...



 


Alessandro Del Piero arrived in New Delhi yesterday. He will embrk on a new adventure with the Indian team Delhi Dynamos, who will participate in the Indian Super League (ISL), the new Indian football league. The 2006 World Cup winner played in Australia with Sydney FC the last two seasons. He signed a three month contract to play in the league whose season ends on 14th December.
 
“It is an exciting challenge for me. This is what I was looking for. I never wanted to go back to where I already played. My goal is to find new challenges, to feel new emotions. The pitch is not the only important thing. The other factors are also important. The most important thing is to connect the sporting side with the role as a football ambassador, being able to represent this marvellous sport I use to play around the world. Football is an essential part of my life ever since I was born. That’s why I decided to play in Australia” said Del Piero on his official website , He added: “That’s also why I chose India. A new league, a new team, new fans, a lot of passion and many people to meet that I hope to win over by playing football, but not only through that”.
